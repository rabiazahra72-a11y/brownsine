School of Thought

What Is Fiqh-e-Jafariya?

Fiqh-e-Jafariya is the school of Islamic jurisprudence named after Imam Ja'far al-Sadiq (A.S.), the sixth Infallible Imam. It is the school of Islamic law followed by Ithna Ashari (Twelver) Shia Muslims, based on the Holy Qur'an, the Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, and the teachings of the Ahlulbayt (A.S.).

The Holy Qur'an

The Holy Qur'an is the primary source of Islamic guidance and the foundation of Fiqh-e-Jafariya.

Sunnah of the Prophet ﷺ

The sayings, actions and approvals of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ explain the teachings of Islam.

Teachings of the Imams

The guidance of the Twelve Infallible Imams (A.S.) preserves and explains the true teachings of Islam.

Reason & Ijtihad

Qualified scholars derive Islamic rulings from the Qur'an, Sunnah and authentic teachings through Ijtihad.

Core Beliefs (Usul al-Din)

☪ Tawhid (Oneness of Allah) +

Allah is One without partner. He alone deserves worship and is the Creator and Sustainer of everything.

⚖ Adl (Divine Justice) +

Allah is absolutely Just and never commits injustice. Every decision of Allah is based on wisdom and justice.

📖 Nubuwwah (Prophethood) +

Allah sent Prophets to guide humanity, with Prophet Muhammad ﷺ being the Final Messenger.

🕌 Imamah (Leadership) +

After Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, leadership continued through the Twelve Infallible Imams (A.S.), beginning with Imam Ali (A.S.).

🌙 Ma'ad (Day of Judgment) +

Every person will be resurrected after death and held accountable before Allah for their deeds.

Amir al-Mu'minin

Imam Ali (A.S.) — The Gate of Knowledge

Imam Ali ibn Abi Talib (A.S.) was the cousin and son-in-law of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ and the first Imam of the Ahlulbayt. He was born inside the Holy Ka'bah and became the greatest example of courage, justice and wisdom.

أَنَا مَدِينَةُ الْعِلْمِ وَعَلِيٌّ بَابُهَا

"I am the City of Knowledge and Ali is its Gate."

  • Title: Amir al-Mu'minin
  • Father: Abu Talib
  • Mother: Fatimah bint Asad
  • Spouse: Lady Fatimah al-Zahra (S.A.)
  • Resting Place: Najaf al-Ashraf, Iraq
Book of Guidance

Nahj al-Balagha

Nahj al-Balagha is a collection of sermons, letters and sayings of Imam Ali (A.S.), compiled by Sayyid Sharif al-Radi. It is regarded as one of the greatest Islamic books after the Holy Qur'an and Hadith.

Main Contents

  • Sermons: Teachings on Tawhid, justice and morality.
  • Letters: Guidance for rulers and society, including the famous letter to Malik al-Ashtar.
  • Sayings: Short but powerful wisdom for everyday life.

"Work for this world as if you will live forever, and work for the Hereafter as if you will die tomorrow."

Holy Cities

Explore the sacred places that hold great spiritual and historical importance for millions of visitors around the world.

Iraq Imam Ali (A.S.)

Najaf Al-Ashraf

Najaf is one of the most respected holy cities in Iraq. It is home to the magnificent shrine of Imam Ali (A.S.) and a worldwide center of Islamic knowledge.

Iraq Imam Hussain (A.S.)

Karbala

Karbala represents the greatest symbol of sacrifice and courage. The shrine of Imam Hussain (A.S.) attracts millions of pilgrims every year, especially during Arbaeen.

Iran Imam Reza (A.S.)

Mashhad

Mashhad is the spiritual heart of Iran and the home of Imam Reza (A.S.) shrine, visited by millions of people from across the world.

Muharram al-Haram

Ziyarat-e-Ashura

Ziyarat-e-Ashura is one of the most well-known salutations to Imam Hussain (A.S.). It is recited especially on the Day of Ashura and throughout the month of Muharram.

This ziyarat expresses love and respect for Imam Hussain (A.S.) and his loyal companions. It reminds believers about the message of Karbala — truth, patience, sacrifice and standing against oppression.

Millions of devotees recite Ziyarat-e-Ashura to remember the sacrifice of Imam Hussain (A.S.) and to seek strength on the path of Ahlulbayt (A.S.).

Guiding the Ummah

Islamic Scholars & Marja'

The scholars of Ahlulbayt (A.S.) have dedicated their lives to preserving Islamic teachings, guiding believers, and spreading the knowledge of the Holy Qur'an and the traditions of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) and his purified family.

Marja-e-Taqlid

Grand Ayatollah Ali al-Sistani

Grand Ayatollah Sayyid Ali al-Sistani is one of the most respected contemporary Shia scholars. Based in Najaf al-Ashraf, he has devoted his life to Islamic jurisprudence, education, and promoting peace, justice, and unity among Muslims.

His religious rulings and scholarly works are followed by millions of believers around the world, making him one of the leading Maraji' of our time.
